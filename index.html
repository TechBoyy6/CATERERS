PUNIT CATERERS

A Legacy of Exceptional Food and Service

Punit Caterers, a renowned name with a rich history spanning generations, has its roots dating back to 1960 when Bhiku Bhai Khimji Rawal made a life-changing move from Rajasthan to settle in the peaceful town of Bordi. For 16 years, he dedicated himself to selflessly serving others at the Sharda Ashram. However, in 1976, a new chapter unfolded as Bhiku Bhai shifted his focus to Dahanu Road.

In 1981, Bhiku Bhai's entrepreneurial spirit took center stage, leading him to establish Punit Caterers in Dahanu Road, where he partnered with his son, Bharat BhikuBhai Rawal. This marked the inception of a culinary legacy that would go on to make a lasting impact in the region. As the years passed, Punit Caterers cultivated a loyal clientele, prompting Bharat Bhai Rawal to broaden their horizons further. In 1994, he opened the Boisar branch, extending their reach to a wider audience and solidifying their reputation for exceptional food and service.

The year 2003 saw the arrival of Purshottam Bhai Sonaji Rawal, affectionately known as Mamaji, who joined the business and took charge of the Boisar branch. His dedication and meticulous attention to detail contributed significantly to the branch's success, reinforcing the family's commitment to excellence.

The legacy seamlessly transitioned into the hands of the next generation. In 2011, Ashish Bharat Rawal, Bharat Rawal's elder son, brought fresh ideas and innovation to the business. In 2020, Sagar Bharat Rawal, the younger son, infused Punit Caterers with youthful energy, further propelling the family business forward.

Throughout their remarkable journey, Punit Caterers has remained steadfast in their commitment to delivering exceptional cuisine and unparalleled service. Each generation's involvement has played a pivotal role in the gradual expansion and prosperity of the family enterprise. Today, Punit Caterers stands as a shining example of the Rawal family's dedication and unwavering commitment. Their passion for crafting remarkable dining experiences has earned them an esteemed place in the hearts of their customers.

As Punit Caterers continues to evolve, it remains firmly rooted in its core values of quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. With each passing year, the legacy of Punit Caterers grows stronger, leaving an indelible impression on the palates and cherished memories of those fortunate enough to experience their exceptional service and delectable cuisine.
